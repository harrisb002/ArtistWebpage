Beatles, The

Number of Images: 45
Profile: A phenomenally successful, and highly influential 1960s British guitar band, which was formed in 1960 in Liverpool, UK.The lineup comprised John Lennon (vocals, guitar, keyboards), Paul McCartney (bass, guitar, keyboards, vocals), George Harrison (guitar, vocals), and Ringo Starr (drums, vocals). Initially a Mersey based 'beat' group with American R&B, rock & roll and country influences, by the mid-1960s the band was experimenting with psychedelia and incorporating innovative production techniques involving tape loops and other effects. The group split-up in 1970, but the former members all pursued solo careers. The band has continued to authorise and release albums of previously unreleased material, best of collections, live albums and remixed projects. Inducted into Rock And Roll Hall of Fame in 1988 (Performer). According to the RIAA, the Beatles are the best-selling band in the United States, with 178 million certified units
